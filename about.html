About Us

Who we are?


Vision Statement

Joy Temple ICGC is founded on biblical principles. Our vision is to establish the house of God, through the development of Model New Testament Christians and churches. God blessed mankind after creation and asked us to be fruitful and multiply. He deposited in us, the seed of greatness. However, this seed of greatness can only grow and flourish on a good fertilized soil with the appropriate illumination.

“No matter what prison you are in, nobody can imprison the power of your imagination”

- Pastor Mensa Otabil (FOUNDER)


We raise effective leaders and help people realize their full potential through activities and services that encourage full participation and increase personal satisfaction. We are committed to serve the lost and needy in our community and nation with joy, train and equip God's people who come to our church to develop and grow into maturity in Christ with excellence, integrity and compassion.

Our Mission and Commitments
  • Every human being is created in the image of likeness of God and must be treated with dignity.
  • Train, encourage and empower God's people through personal growth so they will manifest the character of Christ.
  • Influence society with preaching and demonstration of God's plans and purposes for all mankind as revealed in the Holy Scriptures.
  • Account for our words and actions.
  • Professionally and efficiently operate the church and build as a team.
  • Increase access to communication and information.
  • Support our members to achieve their potential.
  • Provide the best programs for our members and community.


Our Belief
Statement of Belief

We believe that the originality of the Word of God through the Scriptures is infallible, inspired by God, and present the entire divine and final disclosure of God's will for our salvation, faith and life.


God

We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three person: God the Father, God the Son, and God the Holy Spirit.
